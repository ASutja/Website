There has never been a more important time than now to support local wineries, wine-related businesses, and tour guides in the Black Sea region.

The countries of the Black Sea region that boast a common rich cultural heritage of viticulture, winemaking and unique wine production practices still do not have the representation they deserve. Furthermore, today there is a growing touristic interest in the Historic World of Wine, with more travellers seeking new authentic experiences.
